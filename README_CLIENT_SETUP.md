### First Time Setup (2 minutes)

1. **Create Your Account**
   - Click "Sign Up"
   - Enter your school name (e.g., "Silva's Martial Arts Academy")
   - Enter your email
   - Create a password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Click "Sign Up"
   - Check your email to verify your account
   - **Forgot Password?** Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen to reset via email


2. **Add Your First Month**
   - Click "Add New Month"
   - Select the month and year
   - Enter your metrics:
     - **Leads**: Number of inquiries/prospects
     - **Appointments Made**: How many appointments you scheduled
     - **Appointments Showed**: How many actually showed up
     - **Enrollments**: New students who joined
   - Enter your revenue:
     - **PIF**: Paid In Full memberships
     - **Down Payments**: Initial payments for payment plans
     - **Event Revenue**: Tournaments, seminars, etc.
     - **Pro Shop Sales**: Gear, uniforms, equipment
     - **MRR**: Monthly Recurring Revenue
   - Enter student counts:
     - **Students at Start**: Active students at beginning of month
     - **Students at End**: Active students at end of month
   - Click "Save"

3. **View Your Dashboard**

### Understanding Your Metrics

**Conversion Targets:**
- Appointments to Leads: 50% (if you have 100 leads, aim for 50 appointments)
- Showed to Appointments: 80% (if you schedule 50, aim for 40 to show)
- Enrollments to Showed: 80% (if 40 show, aim for 32 enrollments)

**Churn Rate:**
- Shows the percentage of students you're losing
- Lower is better (under 5% is excellent)
- Formula: (Students Lost / Starting Students) Ã— 100
